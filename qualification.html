My personal journey

Focus Areas: Bioprocess Engineering, Synthetic Biology, Biotechnology

During my four-year undergraduate studies, I am building a solid foundation in biological engineering principles, laboratory techniques, and research methodologies. Program: Master's and Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering
