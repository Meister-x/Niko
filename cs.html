Counter-Strike

Counter-Strike is more than just a game to me—it's a passion that has been part of my life since middle school. With over 1700 hours of gameplay, this competitive FPS game has become my top choice for daily leisure and relaxation. The game teaches valuable lessons about strategy, teamwork, quick decision-making, and maintaining composure under pressure—skills that surprisingly translate well into academic and research environments.

What makes Counter-Strike truly special is the social aspect. Teaming up with good friends, coordinating strategies, and celebrating victories together creates memorable experiences and strengthens friendships. Following professional esports tournaments and supporting my favorite teams adds another layer of excitement. This hobby provides a perfect balance to my academic life, offering both mental stimulation and social connection in a competitive digital environment.
